ABOUT ME

Graduated as an Industrial Management Engineering at University of Vigo (UVIGO) in 2022.

During the degree I was chosen for an intership at an European Leader Manufacturer Chimney Company as a part of the General Management Team. Nowadays I continue being part of the Team with a 6 month scholarship.

Thanks to my first contacts with business, I realized how much data is generated and how much potential is wasted.
Emphasizing the importance of its correct transformation and interpretation to obtain indicators and vital information for a successful business development.

Due to all these factors, it sparked my desire to specialize in Data Analytics.
